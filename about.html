Hello, I'm Deanna!

And I make stuff.

I began my artistic endeavors in early childhood, coloring on my bedsheets and blaming my little brother. (My parents saw straight through that one. No way my bro could’ve pulled off that perfectly shaped and shaded sketch of a human eyeball. But I digress…) I gravitated toward all things arty and as a grown adult, ended up focusing more heavily on theatre and music. In an unusual coincidence of events, I took a job some years ago singing onboard a cruise ship. In the fine print, it was stated that I would also be serving as the “Imaging Manager”, and was thus responsible for all of the photography and printables onboard, as well as being the designer, and editor-in-chief of the the daily newspaper. I found myself very excited about this unexpected laundry list of job duties. For years I had dabbled in graphic design, typography, layouts, and the like, and now I had a chance to do it for real! I jumped in feet first and reignited my passion for building tangible art through thoughtfully crafted design.

I pay meticulous attention to detail. I am a quick study and a fantastic problem solver. I love a challenge. I am an avid learner and am constantly enrolled in curriculum to expand my knowledge and skill set.

I love coffee and tacos, and my #1 goal in life is a hammock between two palm trees. So you can rest assured that I'm not looking to add any more stress or drama to our world. You tell me what your goals are, and I'll deliver great design to make them happen. Easy peasy.

And we'll all live happily ever after.

So what are you waiting for?! I’m hella friendly and easy to talk to.

Let's get started!
