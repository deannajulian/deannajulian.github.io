I decided that something sweet and whimsical was the way to go with this logo, considering the inspiration for the name. My dear grandparents, Mimi & Dadoo, were two of the sweetest most genuine folks to ever walk the earth. Not too many years ago, when they were both still here, Mimi and I were playing a round of Yahtzee when Dadoo came by on his way to bed. After 50 some-odd years of marriage, he stopped to kiss Mimi on the cheek. She said to me, "You know, he's my beloved." To which Dadoo replied, "Beloved and true." It was a perfect moment that I hope I never forget.

I figure, if a girl wants to be a legend, she should just go ahead and be one.
